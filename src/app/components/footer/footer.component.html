Prince Sultan Cardiac Center

Cardiac services started in 1979 at Riyadh Military Hospital and ever since have witnessed significant progress.

The center was eventually crowned by the Decree of HRH the late Prince Sultan bin Abdulaziz (May Allah bless him) No. 4228 dated 24/11/1412 AH stating the establishing of Prince Sultan Cardiac Center to act as a specialized healthcare.
